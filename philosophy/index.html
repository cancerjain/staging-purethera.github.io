Beyond the product

Pure TheraPro Rx is a family-owned and run business. The company was initially formed as the products division of a multi-modality medical clinic in Colorado. Over many years, we realized that most of the food consumed and the supplements people were taking were highly ineffective and, in some cases, a waste of time and money. We also realized the current Standard American Diet (SAD) consists of processed, genetically altered, and nutritionally void foods. We could not be passive in this “new” reality and decided the most effective method to change was through pure, carefully sourced, evidence-based supplementation.

Sourcing only the best ingredients
from all over the world.

We ensure that each ingredient lives up to our purity standards.

Meticulous ingredient selection, potency, purity, manufacturer certifications (NSF, cGMP, FDA Inspected), patented ingredients, independent third-party analysis, China-free sourcing and clinical studies; these are but a few of the measures Pure TheraPro Rx® requires to ensure the consistent quality and effectiveness of our formulas. This is what customers worldwide expect from us. All Pure TheraPro Rx brand supplements are 100% made in the USA and exclusively sold through our managed channels.

Pure to you supplements.

Direct to Consumer as it should be.

Don’t sacrifice your purity with third-hand supplements. Many supplement companies rely on several layers of expenses that inevitably reduce the quality or quantity of raw material for the sake of profit.

Finally, the retailer / doctor / clinic must make their profit margin, so the price is inflated yet again. The result is a substantially inferior product (fillers / flow agents / junk) at a much higher cost to you.

In order to bring you the best possible quality at the lowest possible price we chose a different route - direct to you, we are both the manufacturer and retailer. The result is a more pure catalogue of products at a better cost to you.

Did you know?

The typical company is forced to buy raw material as cheap as possible to account for the following items:
  1. supply/manufacturing costs
  2. overhead costs
  3. marketing costs
  4. wholesale profit margin selling to retailers / doctors / clinics
